#Project Title

Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Dashboard
#project tittle

project obective
the main objective of the vrinda sales analysis dashboard is to analyze sales data andgenerate meaningful business insights using tools like excel,power BI.the dashboard helps management understand customer behaviour,sales performance and business growth.

#Dataset
Vrinda Store Sales Data
This dataset is used to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, and business growth opportunities.

Which age group purchases the most products?

Which category has the highest order quantity?
Channel KPIs

Project Insights
Customer Insights
Women customers contribute a higher percentage of total sales compared to men.
Adult age group customers are the major buyers in the store.
Top-performing states contribute a significant share of revenue.
Sales Insights
Sales peak during festive or seasonal months.
Some months show comparatively lower sales, indicating off-season demand.
Product Insights
Certain product categories dominate overall revenue generation.
High-demand products can be prioritized for inventory management.
Channel Insights
Online marketplaces like Amazon and Flipkart contribute the majority of sales.
Identifying the best-performing platform helps optimize marketing strategies.
Operational Insights
Most orders are successfully delivered.
A small percentage of cancellations and returns impacts revenue

#Final Conclusion

The Vrinda Store Sales Analysis project helps understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and sales trends. The analysis shows that women and adult customers are the primary contributors to sales revenue. Online channels such as Amazon and Flipkart play a major role in driving business growth.

By focusing on high-performing states, popular product categories, and successful sales channels, Vrinda Store can improve marketing strategies, inventory planning, and customer targeting. Reducing cancellations and improving delivery efficiency can further enhance customer satisfaction and profitability.

Overall, this dashboard provides valuable business insights that support data-driven decision-making and future sales growth.
